Welcome to My Gundam World

This site showcases a collection of visual works featuring Gundam model kits. Gundam is an internationally renowned franchise spanning around 83 animated series and movies, starting from 1979. Each model represents hours of careful assembly, painting, and photography.

The Gundam franchise has captivated audiences for decades with its complex storytelling, mecha designs, and philosophical themes. Building these models is both a technical challenge and an artistic expression.

Gundam Gallery

Explore my collection of assembled and customized Gundam models. Each piece represents a unique build with attention to detail, painting techniques, and photographic composition.

Buildong process

I build Gundam models as a passionate hobby, combining technical assembly with artistic photography. Each kit begins as hundreds of plastic pieces on runners, requiring careful cutting, sanding, and assembly.

The process continues with panel lining to enhance details, custom painting for unique color schemes, and sometimes weathering effects for battle-worn appearances. The final step is photography - capturing each model in dynamic poses with proper lighting to showcase the craftsmanship.

This hobby teaches patience, attention to detail, and creative problem-solving. It's a meditative process that results in tangible works of art inspired by one of animation's most enduring franchises.
